The Fastest Popcorn Chicken

You might think it's store bought, because it is

I'm not going to lie, I have no idea where the nearest KFC is. But I do know that Fred Meyer (the local Kroger chain) has some amazing frozen popcorn chicken. Serve it up with a nice big salad on the side and you've got yourself a somewhat respectable meal.

The Ingredients:

  • A few good handfuls of frozen popcorn chicken

The Steps:

  1. If needed, buy a bag of frozen popcorn chicken
  2. Preheat oven to 425
  3. Put chicken on a jelly roll pan (something with edges)
  4. Bake for 6-8 minutes
  5. Take out and flip each piece
  6. Bake again for 6-8 minutes
  7. Serve with sauces of choice, and a nice salad

Notes:

Don't crowd the pieces, or they won't cook as fast. Be sure to turn them even though it's tedious.
